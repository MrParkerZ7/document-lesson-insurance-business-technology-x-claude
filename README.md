# Insurance Business Technology Curriculum

A comprehensive learning path for **IT Developers** and **Product Owners** working in the insurance industry.

---

## Who Is This For?

### IT Developers
- Backend/frontend developers building insurance platforms
- API designers and integration specialists
- DevOps engineers and QA engineers

### Product Owners
- POs managing insurance product development
- Business analysts and technical product managers
- Scrum masters working with insurance teams

---

## Quick Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **FNOL** | First Notice of Loss - initial claim report |
| **MGA** | Managing General Agent - underwriting authority |
| **TPA** | Third Party Administrator for claims |
| **UBI** | Usage-Based Insurance (telematics) |
| **Loss Ratio** | Claims paid / Premiums earned |
| **Endorsement** | Modification to an existing policy |
| **Rider** | Additional coverage added to base policy |
| **Subrogation** | Right to recover from third party after claim |

> See individual lessons for complete glossaries and detailed explanations.
