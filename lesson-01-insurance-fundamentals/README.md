# Lesson 01: Insurance Business Fundamentals

## Overview

This lesson introduces the foundational concepts of insurance business, stakeholders, value chain, and business model.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to:
- Understand what insurance is and how it works
- Identify key stakeholders in the insurance ecosystem
- Explain the insurance value chain
- Understand the insurance business model and revenue streams
